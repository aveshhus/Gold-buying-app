# 🚀 Ultra Simple Deployment Guide

## 📖 What You're Doing

You're putting your app on the internet so people can use it.

**Think of it like:**
- Your computer = Your house (where you build the app)
- Hostinger server = A shop (where people can access it)
- We're moving your app from your house to the shop

---

## ✅ Step 1: Make Sure Cursor is Connected

### What to Do:

1. **Close Cursor completely**
2. **Open Cursor again**
3. **Wait 10 seconds**

**That's it!** Cursor is now connected to Hostinger.

---

## ✅ Step 2: Ask Cursor to Help You Deploy

### What to Do:

1. **Press `Ctrl + L`** (opens Cursor chat)
2. **Type this exactly:**

```
Help me deploy my GoldApp to my Hostinger VPS.
My server IP is 93.127.206.164
My temporary domain is srv1226397.hstgr.cloud
Walk me through each step with exact commands.
```

3. **Press Enter**

**Cursor will tell you what to do next!**

---

## ✅ Step 3: Follow Cursor's Instructions

### Cursor Will Tell You:

**Step 1:** "First, connect to your server..."
- Copy the command Cursor gives you
- Paste it in your terminal/command prompt
- Press Enter

**Step 2:** "Now install Node.js..."
- Copy the command
- Paste and run it

**Step 3:** "Upload your code..."
- Cursor will tell you how

**And so on...**

**Just copy-paste what Cursor tells you!**

---

## 📋 What Cursor Will Help You With

### 1. **Installing Software**
   - Cursor gives you: `apt install nodejs`
   - You copy-paste it
   - Done!

### 2. **Configuring Files**
   - Cursor tells you: "Create .env file"
   - Cursor shows you what to put in it
   - You copy-paste it

### 3. **Starting Your App**
   - Cursor gives you: `pm2 start...`
   - You copy-paste it
   - Your app is running!
